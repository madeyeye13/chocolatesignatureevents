Let's Answer Your Questions

How much does event planning cost?

The cost of an event planning can vary depending on the size and type of the event as well as the location.

How far in advance should I book my event?

It is best to book an event planner as soon as possible, in fact immediately you are sure of the event date especially if you are planning a large or complex event.

Event planners book up several months or even a year in advance.

What happens on the day of the event?

On the day of the event, the event planner will oversee all aspects of the event, including managing the vendors, coordinating entertainment and handling any issue that may arise. They will work to ensure the event runs smoothly and that the guests have a great experience.

Are you the one to do the make-up and cake bake?

A professional event planner is not a decorator or cake vendor or a make-up artist. I can only work side by side with my vendors.

What is the role of the client in the event planning?

Now the client does nothing other than to sit and enjoy his or her event.

The event planner will work closely with the client to ensure that their vision for the event is achieved.
