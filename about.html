Chocolate Signature Events

founded by the visionary Maryam Haruna, is the epitome of elegance and sophistication in wedding and event planning. As the Principal Experience Manager and CEO, Maryam brings her well-rounded expertise and passion for creating unforgettable experiences to every event.

The company's mission is driven by Maryam's unwavering commitment to excellence, creativity, and personalized service. We specialize in curating luxurious and bespoke events tailored to your unique vision and needs. Whether it's a grand wedding, an intimate social gathering, or a corporate affair, our dedicated team ensures that every detail is meticulously planned and executed to perfection.

Maryam's vision for Chocolate Signature Events is to be the leading event planning company known for transforming dreams into reality. We aim to set new standards in the industry by consistently delivering exceptional and memorable events that exceed our clients' expectations. We strive to create experiences that are not just events but cherished memories.
